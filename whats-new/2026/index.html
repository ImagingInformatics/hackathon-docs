What's new in 2026?

✈️ SIIM26 is in Pittsburgh, PA (June 10-12)!

Heading to SIIM this year? The annual meeting is in Pittsburgh, PA from June 10-12. As always, the hackathon runs in hybrid format so you can participate fully virtually or join us in person. Registration and housing for SIIM26 are now open at the SIIM26 registration page.

Pathologists and imagers, take note: the SIIM-DPA Bridge Pass gives you access to both SIIM26 and PathVisions26. The deadline to register with the Bridge Pass is May 29 so act soon!

📝️ A Registration Form

Why a registration form?!? SIIM Hackathons have always been a casual experience and it remains so. However, you are highly encouraged to use this registration form so we can reliably share announcements and updates. Help us help you get the most out of your SIIM Hackathon experience!

💡️ A New Idea Pitch Form

The freedom to pick and choose your projects that you know and love at the SIIM hackathon will always be there.

But for those who prefer a more structured approach, or want to browse ideas currently being mulled for this year's hackathon, we have an idea pitch form where you can suggest ideas and projects to tackle this year and nominate a team lead (yourself or someone else).
