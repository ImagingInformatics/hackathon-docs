# What's new in 2026?

## ✈️ SIIM26 is in Pittsburgh, PA (June 10-12)!
Heading to SIIM this year? The annual meeting is in Pittsburgh, PA from June 10-12. As always, the hackathon runs in hybrid format so you can participate fully virtually or join us in person. Registration and housing for SIIM26 are now open at the [SIIM26 registration page](https://annualmeeting.siim.org/register/).

Pathologists and imagers, take note: the [SIIM-DPA Bridge Pass](https://annualmeeting.siim.org/siim-dpa-bridge-pass-siim26-pathvisions26/) gives you access to both SIIM26 and PathVisions26. The deadline to register with the Bridge Pass is **May 29** so act soon!


## 📝️ A [Registration Form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/55e079d9c30c40deb4ae7b6805419804)
Why a registration form?!? SIIM Hackathons have always been a casual experience and it remains so. However, you are highly encouraged to use this [registration form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/55e079d9c30c40deb4ae7b6805419804) so we can reliably share announcements and updates. Help us help you get the most out of your SIIM Hackathon experience!


## 💡️ A New Idea Pitch Form
The freedom to pick and choose your projects that you know and love at the SIIM hackathon will always be there.

But for those who prefer a more structured approach, or want to browse ideas currently being mulled for this year's hackathon, we have an [idea pitch form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScZPLy4obTYKS30FEWGDH-9FfzG2w4WJkKVZWbUZjHEf1Mjmg/viewform?usp=publish-editor) where you can suggest ideas and projects to tackle this year and nominate a team lead (yourself or someone else).
